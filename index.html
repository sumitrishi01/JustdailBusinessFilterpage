Experience a Whole New Style of Living With the Help of Hotels in Mumbai

People enjoy staying in hotels for a variety of reasons. Staying in a hotel allows one to escape their daily routine, allowing them to relax and disconnect from the hustle and bustle of city life. Another reason why people prefer hotels is the convenience they provide. Hotels provide a variety of amenities and services, such as room service, housekeeping, indoor restaurants, pools, gaming zones, gyms, and so on, making it convenient for guests to enjoy everything under one roof. Thus, hotels in Mumbai play a major role for locals as well as for those who are travelling from other cities or countries.

Some Fun Facts About Hotels

Hotels have a long history, with some of the earliest examples discovered in ancient Europe and Eastern Asia. These hotels were known to provide basic lodging for travellers. Hotels began to evolve into more sophisticated establishments during the Middle Ages. They were commonly found near pilgrimage or trade routes. Many monasteries and convents would provide lodging to travellers, and it was later discovered that some private homes had begun to accept visitors as well.

The hotel industry grew rapidly in the twentieth century. As a result, we saw the rise of chain hotels where you could find various amenities such as air conditioning, elevators, and in-room televisions, among other things. Today, the top hotels come in a number of different forms, from budget motels to luxury resorts, and they cater to a wide range of travellers for both business and leisure purposes.

What are the Types of Hotels?

When you will conduct a search for hotels near me, you will find a plethora of options. It can be quite intimidating to select one hotel out of so many options. Below are some most common types of hotels:

A chain hotel is one that is part of a larger group owned and managed by a single company. Each hotel in the chain usually operates under the same brand. For example, Oyo hotels fall under this type of hotel. You can easily find the same by conducting a search on Oyo hotels near me.

A bed and breakfast hotel, also known as a B&B hotel, is a type of lodging that is typically small. The term ‘bed’ in B&B hotels refers to overnight accommodations, which are typically fully furnished rooms with all of the basic amenities to spend the night comfortably. The term ‘breakfast’ refers to the complimentary breakfast that is usually included in the room rate. By conducting a search for ‘hotels near me for stay’ , you will find various options with B&B hotels included.

The ideal target audience of this type of hotel are travellers. Thus, you will often find these lodging located at the roadside of highways and other such routes. They also offer parking space, which is the main reason for the attraction of its guests. A motel will have a few guest rooms and basic amenities. These hotels are considered cheap, so when you search for ‘cheap hotels near me’ you will find various motels in your list of options.

A resort is a type of commercial establishment that provides a variety of services and amenities to guests with the primary goal of providing leisure and recreational activities. Restaurants, swimming pools, sports facilities, spas, and entertainment venues are common amenities at resorts. Resorts are located in appealing places such as beaches, mountains, or other scenic locations. Resorts can range in size depending on what type of resort they are in. Resorts are considered the best type of hotels, thus when you search for 'best hotels near me', you will come across many resorts in your list of options.

A guest house is a type of lodging establishment that provides accommodation in a private home. They usually have a more homely atmosphere than a hotel. Guest houses typically are run by a family or an owner and a small staff. They may offer shared spaces such as living rooms, kitchens, or outdoor areas, and may include breakfast or other meals. Guest houses can be found in a variety of locations, from urban neighbourhoods to rural areas.

Also known as a holiday home, this type of hotel is a vacation cottage or property, which is given on rent to guests for short-term stays. They are fully furnished houses that provide guests with a comfortable stay during the holidays.

Like motels, roadhouse hotels are situated beside a roadway to attract passing guests for overnight stays. They, too, offer all kinds of amenities and food services. When one is on a road trip, one may often come across a roadhouse.

These days, most of these hotels are couple friendly. However, if you wish to cross-check or specifically search for hotels that are couple-friendly, we advise our readers to conduct a search with keywords like ‘couple friendly hotels near me’ or ‘hotels near me for couples.’

What are the Types of Rooms in Hotels?

Each hotel will have a different type of hotel that best meets the needs of its guests. However, after doing some research, we have found the following rooms to be commonly found in almost every hotel.

Single Bed Room: Just like the name suggests, this room comes with a single bed meant for the use of a single person.

Double Bed Room: Like a single bedroom, this room comes with a double bed and is meant for accommodating two people at a time.

Queen and King-Sized Bed Room: These rooms are bigger in size in comparison to single and double bedrooms. This is because the bed provided to the guest comes in queen or king size, which requires quite some space.

Connecting Rooms: These rooms will at first look like regular rooms that have normal individual entrances. But on the inside, you will see that these rooms are connected through a passage or a door.

Suite: These rooms are considered luxurious rooms as they have a living room connected to one or more bedrooms. The bed and furniture used in these rooms are always of high quality.

To know more, we suggest our readers to look for 'hotels near me for stay' or 'best hotels near me.'

What Does the ‘Star’ Rating Signify in Hotels?

The 'star' rating system is a widely recognised system for indicating a hotel's quality and level of service. An independent organisation or government agency typically determines the rating by evaluating various aspects of the hotel, such as the quality of its rooms, amenities, service, cleanliness, and location.

In general, the number of stars awarded to a hotel relates to the level of luxury and amenities provided by them. The greater the number of stars, the greater will be the quality of service. A one-star hotel, for example, may only provide basic amenities, whereas a five-star hotel is likely to provide more luxurious amenities such as fine dining, spas, and extensive leisure facilities. People usually search for ‘3-star hotels near me’ when looking for a hotel within their budget yet with all the basic amenities. While some may look for when looking for a hotel within their budget yet with all the basic amenities. While some may look for ‘5-star hotels near me’ to get luxurious amenities and the best services during their stay.

However, it is important to note that the rating system is not standardised across countries or regions, and different rating systems may use different criteria for every place. Furthermore, the star rating is not always an accurate indicator of a hotel's quality, as a hotel with a lower star rating can still provide excellent service and amenities.

What Amenities can be Found in a Hotel?

The amenities found in a hotel can vary depending on the hotel's star rating and type, however, we have done some research and found the following to be the most common amenities that guests can expect to find in most hotels.

A bed with clean blankets and pillows - available in all types of hotels.

A private bathroom with a shower or bathtub. They also provide fresh towels and toiletries. These are available in all types of hotels.

A television with cable or satellite connection to view various channels during your stay. This facility is available in 3-star, 4-star and 5-star hotels.

A telephone to call room service. This is available in 3-star, 4-star and 5-star hotels.

A desk or table and a dressing table with a chair - available in 4-star and 5-star hotels.

Free WiFi - available in all types of hotels. Some may not charge you for this service, while some hotels may charge on a data usage basis.

Choice of ordering room service or making use of the on-site dining facility - available in 3-star, 4-star and 5-star hotels.

Parking space for guests is available in most hotels, but it is advised to check before you do a reservation.

Housekeeping services are available in all types of hotels.

24-hour front reception- Most of the hotels have a 24-hour front reception or a caretaker.

Gyms or fitness centres - available in 4-star and 5-star hotels.

Swimming pools - available in 4-star and 5-star hotels.

Spa - available in 4-star or 5-star hotels.

To avail of any of these services and amenities or to simply take a break from your daily life, we urge readers to look for 'best hotels near me.' A comfortable stay in a hotel may just be the right solution to your current stressful life.

Things to Keep in Mind When Booking your Hotel Stay

When booking a room in a hotel, there are several things to consider:

Location: Take into account the hotel's location and proximity to the places such as any tourist attractions or business venues that you wish to see.

When booking a room at a hotel, you must always take into account their charges for per night. By comparing the budget and price of the room, you will come to know which hotel room suits best your needs and your pocket. You can search for ‘cheap hotels near me’ when trying to find a hotel that best suits your budget.

Amenities: Because you are paying a fee to stay at a hotel, you should think about the amenities they provide. To name a few, always look for free Wi-Fi, breakfast, a gym or pool, and so on. You should select a hotel that is within your budget and has all of the amenities you require during your stay.

Reviews: To get a sense of the hotel's reputation and quality of services, read their online reviews from previous guests. This will help you understand if the hotel you are looking at is the best choice or not.

Room size and type: When you are looking for a hotel, also look at their types of rooms because this will help you understand which type of room is best suited for you and your needs. For example, if you are going on a trip with family or friends, you would want to book a room that is larger in size or connected to each other.

Policies: Check the hotel's policies, such as check-in and check-out timings, cancellation policies, and any additional fees, to avoid any surprises to your total bill at the end of your stay. For unmarried couples, it is always wise to check if a hotel is couple friendly. The best way to see which hotels are couple friendly is by conducting a search on ‘couple friendly hotels near me’ or ‘hotels near me for couples.’

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the check-in and check-out time in hotels?

Every hotel has its check-in and check-out timing. It is thus advised to speak with a hotel representative in advance to avoid any kind of inconvenience.

2. Is the parking space free of cost in hotels?

Most hotels provide free parking fees or have their charges included in the room rate. However, some hotels may charge you separately for the same. Thus, it is always advised to cross-check the same with the hotel of your choice.

3. Do hotels charge for Wi-Fi or internet access?

Usually, Wi-Fi or internet is given to guests free of cost or included in their room rate. However, we advise readers to always cross-check the same.

4. Are hotels pet friendly?

These days, many hotels are pet friendly but this may not be the case for all hotels. Thus, it is always wise to ask and confirm the same.

5. Are any documents required during check-in?

Yes, hotels will ask for government ID proof such as an Aadhar card during your check-in. For people who travel from abroad, their passport and Visa may be asked during check-in.
